Hi, how we support you?

No matter the question, we've got the answers.

TradEdge is a comprehensive digital platform designed to organize, enumerate, and empower the informal sector in Nigeria and beyond. We bring together traders, artisans, farmers, transporters, and small business owners onto a single, secure system. Our solution provides digital identity, access to banking services, financial inclusion, business development tools, and government partnership opportunities — all tailored to strengthen the informal economy.

TradEdge is open to all categories of the informal sector, including traders, artisans, farmers, transporters, small-scale manufacturers, and cooperatives. If you belong to the informal sector and want to be recognized, supported, and financially included, TradEdge is for you.

  • Digital Identity & Recognition
  • Financial Inclusion & instant bank account
  • Access to Credit & Loans
  • Exposure to new Market Access
  • Government Support & Grants
  • Business Growth Tools & Data Insights
  • Insurance & Security options

Registration is simple and can be done through our trained field agents (enumerators) or via self-registration on our website www.tradedge.ng. A mobile app will be available soon.

We require your basic and business details. To generate a working bank account, your NIN is needed. Additional KYC details may be requested to upgrade your account tier.

All personal and financial data is encrypted with advanced AES technology. We comply with NDPR and international standards. Your data will never be sold or shared without your consent.

Basic registration is free. Some services like insurance, loans, or premium features may attract small fees, all communicated transparently.

We partner with Sterling Bank in Nigeria. Once verified, you automatically get a bank account to receive payments, access loans, save securely, and grow your business.
