About TradEdge

Empowering Markets. Transforming Businesses. Driving Growth.

At TradEdge, we believe that markets are vibrant ecosystems that drive economic empowerment and growth. Our mission is to digitize, simplify, and revolutionize how businesses and market stakeholders operate, connect, and thrive. By bridging the gap between traditional trade practices and modern digital innovations, we empower traders, businesses, associations, and governments with tools for transparency, efficiency, and growth.

Learn more

What We Do

At TradEdge, we are transforming how markets and businesses operate by providing solutions such as:

Enumeration & Digital Identity

Secure digital profiling of traders, farmers, associations, and other stakeholders.

Revenue Collection & Monitoring

Efficient systems that reduce leakages and enhance accountability.

Financial Inclusion

Seamless integration with banks and fintechs for access to accounts, loans, and credit.

Business Growth Tools

Market data, analytics, and resources to drive expansion.

Government Partnerships

Transparent solutions that support revenue generation and economic planning.
